Represent Natural Forces

I created a continuous snowstorm with velocity, acceleration, and gravity where the user can customize their gravity value through a slider. I referenced existing examples on openprocessing.org and applied content from our lecture to implement this.

I was trying to exaggerate aspects of a snowstorm by having it release snow in "groups" rather than continuously smooth, and having a slider rather than a text input box allows the user to easily adjust their gravity value.
